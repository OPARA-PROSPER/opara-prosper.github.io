WHAT IS ENACTUS?

Enactus is an international non-profit organisation that brings together student, academic and business leaders who are committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to improve the quality of life and standard of living for people in need.

OUR REACH

en . act . us

Entrepreneurial-initiating business innovation with integrity and passion.

Action-the experience of social impact that sparks social enterprise.

US-student, academic and business leaders collaborating to create a better world.

WHAT WE DO

We believe investing in students who take entrepreneurial action for others creates a better world for us all.
Enactus futo projects are focused on the people, planet, and profit. We strive to carry out projects that affect lives Economically, socially and yet in an Environmentally Sustainasble Way.

PROJECTS

I-Perceive: this is a project that simply delves into the problems of our target audience who at one time or the other have experienced some form of marginalization, and then foster long term solutions by awakening and planting seeds of hope, through an entrepreneurial approach.

Agrovation: the project started in 2004 with a goal of tackling the problem of unemployment using agriculture, while improving the Agricultural value chain in Imo State. The project is focused on utilizing varieties of crops to start up businesses for her target audience.
